How Emome Calculates Scores

  • Inputs: you provide an emotional tone (1–5) and choose how long the interaction lasted.
  • Weighted score: Emome multiplies your emotional tone by a duration weight (short = 1, medium = 2, long = 3). The result ranges from 1 to 15.
  • Consistency: your relationship average smooths individual scores over time, so one off‑day won’t ruin an otherwise positive relationship.
  • Visualisation: the radar dot moves outward as scores climb, helping you see at a glance how healthy an interaction was. Over time a full radar map reveals the health of all your connections.
