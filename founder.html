Our Story

Emome was born from heartbreak. A few years ago I went through one of the toughest periods of my life — navigating a close friendship that had turned toxic. Each meeting left me anxious and drained, yet I kept doubting my own feelings. Were things really that bad or was I overreacting?

So I started writing down notes about every time we hung out: who I was with, how long it lasted and, most importantly, how it made me feel. Weeks later a pattern emerged: nearly every interaction left me with a negative score. Seeing it in black‑and‑white was the wake‑up call I needed.

“I realised our emotions are data points — if only we capture them, we can decode the health of our relationships.”

That experience inspired Emome. I built this emotional radar so no one else has to feel lost in a sea of feelings. By logging and scoring your interactions, Emome helps you discover which relationships uplift you and which quietly erode your wellbeing.

Emome is a product of Antare Inc., a company I founded to innovate in the emotional wellness space. Named after one of the brightest stars in the night sky, Antare symbolises guidance — just as I hope Emome will guide you toward more fulfilling relationships.

— Ruben Arena
