Features

Dive into the tools that make Emome unique. Each feature is designed to help you understand, quantify and improve your relationships.

Emotional Logging

Quickly jot down every interaction: who it was with, how long you spent and how it made you feel. Emome makes journaling your emotions effortless.

Weighted Scoring

Receive instant feedback with Uber‑style scores that account for emotional intensity and the time you spent. Recent events count more, so your insights stay fresh.

Relationship Radar

Visualize all your connections on a dynamic radar map. Spot which relationships are thriving and which need attention at a glance.

Timeline View

Scroll through a chronological timeline of your interactions. See your emotional journey unfold across days, weeks and months.

AI Summaries & Alerts

Let Emome’s AI surface trends and themes from your logs and warn you when toxic patterns emerge. Get personalised insights to stay ahead of trouble.

100% Private & Encrypted

Your logs belong to you alone. End‑to‑end encryption ensures every entry remains confidential. Not even our team can read your data.
